    I am a trained Pediatrician with extensive experience in NICU( New Born care), General Pediatrics & Telemedicine.Currently, I am working as Pediatric Consultant at my private clinic in the Borivali area of Mumbai & Wockhardt Hospital Mira Road, Mumbai Area, India.
